More arithmetic operators

While +, -, *, and / are the most frequently used numeric operators,
there are two others that can be quite helpful:

  • //     calculates the integer value when you divide the left operand by the right operand (also called floor or integer division)
  • %     calculates the remainder when you divide the left operand by the right operand (also called modulus)

Example

Suppose the number of cans of soda you need
is stored in cans_of_soda-needed

To calculate the number of six packs and single cans you need:

six_packs = cans_of_soda-needed // 6
single_cans = cans_of_soda-needed % 6
