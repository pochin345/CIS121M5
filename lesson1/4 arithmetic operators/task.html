Arithmetic operators

An operator is a special symbol placed between two expressions (called operands) that performs a special task.

In 3 type, we saw how the + operator worked.
Now we'll explore some other numeric operators

  • +     adds the two operands
  • -     subtracts the right operand from the left operand
  • /     divides the left operand by the right operand
  • *     multiplies the two operands
